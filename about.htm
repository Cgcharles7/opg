About This Project

This site is a collection of mathematical conjectures and related definitions, and theorems that come from related Wikipedia pages. Each conjecture is stored in a simple JSON format to make the data accessible, searchable, and reusable by others.

About Me

I’m Charles Glover, a mathematician and researcher interested in open mathematical knowledge and data-driven exploration of conjectures. My profession is a Data Scientist at Booz Allen Hamiltion. This project began as an experiment in structuring and preserving open problems, but it’s also a platform for collaboration and independent research.
